History

TechCon was first introduced in Ghana with the aim of encouraging the youth to learn a skill to boost their portfolio and professional backgorund.IT started with 14 members on board including the executives in 2019. It then made it first appearance on GTV which made a great impact by acquiring 2 investors and 7 more participants willing to expand their field.

Mission

Our mission is to reduce Ghana's youth unemployment rate by 15% in the next 5-10years.
